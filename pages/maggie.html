Maggie in 2 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 maggi packet
  • 1 masala maker
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 green chilli
  • as per taste Salt
  • 1/4 tsp haldi powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. First of all, add some water to the boil and boil it to 90 percent.

  2. Now pour oil in the pan and add all the vegetables. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and add salt and fry it for 1 minute.

  3. Now add boiled Maggi and add a little water, with which you add Maggi masala and cook for 1 minute.

  4. Ready to serve.
