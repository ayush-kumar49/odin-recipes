Maggie in 2 minutes

Description

Craving something delicious and quick? This 2-minute Maggi recipe is the ultimate comfort food that’s ready in no time! Made with instant noodles, flavorful tastemaker, and a touch of your favorite add-ins like veggies, cheese, or spices—this recipe is perfect for students, late-night cravings, or busy days. Watch how to turn a simple pack of Maggi into a satisfying, hot bowl of yum in just minutes!

Ingredients

  • 1 maggi packet
  • 1 masala maker
  • 1 onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 green chilli
  • as per taste Salt
  • 1/4 tsp haldi powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tsp oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. First of all, add some water to the boil and boil it to 90 percent.

  2. Now pour oil in the pan and add all the vegetables. Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder and add salt and fry it for 1 minute.

  3. Now add boiled Maggi and add a little water, with which you add Maggi masala and cook for 1 minute.

  4. Ready to serve.
