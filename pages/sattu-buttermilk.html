2 Minutes Sattu Buttermilk

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp sattu
  • 1/4 cup whisked curd
  • 1/4 tsp roasted cumin powder
  • 1 dash grated ginger or ginger paste
  • As needed salt
  • Few Mint/coriander leaves chopped optional
  • As needed chat masala
  • 1 pinch hing

Cooking Instructions

  1. First mix curd and sattu in the glass.

  2. Add spices and salt. Add water as needed to adjust the consistency.

  3. Sprinkle some chat masala.

  4. It's ready to be served.
